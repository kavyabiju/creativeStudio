About Us

Aids centre is basically a home for aids patients. They are given food and place to live. This is run by sngc institutions . The people who are alone or don't have someone to take care can live in these places.Also focuses on HIV and AIDS awareness and prevention among women and girls.
The oraganisation also do the following:
1)Podcast
Make a podcast about HIV and AIDS to inform listeners and motivate them to take action. Share the podcast on the websites of your company and any other social media sites, such as Facebook and Twitter.
2) write and publish a blog
Share your thoughts on NWGHAAD or what HIV prevention means to you in a blog post. Any subject that focuses on HIV and AIDS among women and girls is acceptable for the blog post. Post links to your blog on Twitter, Facebook, and other social media sites.
3) virtual walk
Encourage your loved ones, friends, and neighbours to participate in a walk or run in support of NWGHAAD. On March 10, in honour of the 17 years the Office on Women's Health has held NWGHAAD, challenge them to 17 minutes of walking, running, or exercise repetitions! To avoid contracting COVID, always wear your mask and follow any local laws on social distance.
